Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how DocShift collects, uses, protects, and shares your personal information when you use our document conversion platform.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and account credentials when you register
Document Files: Files you upload for conversion, processing, or editing through our platform
Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and how you interact with our services
Payment Information: Billing details and transaction history for premium subscriptions
Communication Data: Messages, feedback, and support requests you send to us

2. How We Use Your Information

Process and convert your documents using our AI-powered tools
Provide, maintain, and improve our document conversion services
Manage your account, subscriptions, and billing
Send service updates, security alerts, and support communications
Analyze usage patterns to enhance platform performance and user experience
Prevent fraud, abuse, and ensure platform security

3. Document Processing & Storage

Uploaded files are processed using secure, encrypted servers
Documents are temporarily stored only for the duration of processing
Processed files are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours
We do not access, read, or analyze the content of your documents
Premium users can choose to store documents securely for extended periods

4. Information Sharing

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information or documents
Third-party service providers may process data under strict confidentiality agreements
Cloud storage providers (AWS, Google Cloud) for secure infrastructure
Payment processors for subscription and billing management
Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

End-to-end encryption for all file uploads and downloads
SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
Multi-factor authentication available for enhanced account security
Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring and access controls

6. Your Privacy Rights

Access: Request copies of your personal data we hold
Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time

7. Cookies & Tracking

Essential cookies for platform functionality and user sessions
Analytics cookies to understand how you use our services
Preference cookies to remember your settings and choices
You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings

8. Data Retention

Account information retained while your account is active
Uploaded documents deleted within 24 hours unless otherwise specified
Usage logs retained for 12 months for security and improvement purposes
Billing records kept as required by law (typically 7 years)
Data deleted within 30 days of account closure unless legally required

9. International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries where our service providers operate
We ensure adequate protection through approved transfer mechanisms
Standard Contractual Clauses and adequacy decisions govern transfers
Your data receives the same level of protection regardless of location

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately
Parents can contact us if they believe we have collected their child's information

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time
Significant changes will be communicated via email or platform notifications
Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance
